# Privacy Policy for Notiq Extension

## Introduction

Notiq is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles your information.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Store
- **Your notes content** - stored locally in your browser
- **Extension settings** - color preferences and toggle states
- **Note metadata** - creation dates and timestamps

### Storage Location
All your notes and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not have access to your personal data.

## Permissions Explanation

### Required Permissions:
- **`storage`** - to save your notes and settings locally in your browser
- **`activeTab`** - to display sticky notes on your current webpage
- **`scripting`** - to enable note functionality and interaction
- **`tabs`** - to communicate with the extension when you switch between pages
- **`<all_urls>`** - to work on all websites where you want to use notes

### What We Do NOT Do:
- We do not share your data with third parties
- We do not track your browsing history or monitor your activity
- We do not access your personal information, passwords, or form data

## Data Usage

### How We Use Permissions:
- Display sticky notes on web pages you visit
- Save and retrieve your notes from local browser storage
- Maintain note functionality when you navigate between pages
- Remember your preferences across browser sessions

## Your Control

You can:
- Delete individual notes by clicking on them in the popup
- Clear all data by uninstalling the extension
- Disable the extension at any time
- Review the source code (extension is open for inspection)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the extension update.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the extension's support channel.
